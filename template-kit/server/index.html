Answers to Your Most Common Questions

What is a VPN and why do I need it?

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) encrypts your internet connection, hides your IP address, and protects your privacy—keeping you safe from hackers, trackers, and surveillance.

Do you keep any logs of my activity?

No. We have a strict no-log policy. Your browsing history, data, and activity are never stored or shared.

Can I use the VPN on multiple devices?

Yes! A single account lets you connect across multiple devices—laptops, phones, tablets, and even smart TVs.

 

Will VPN slow down my internet?

Our optimized servers ensure fast and stable speeds, so you can stream, game, and browse without interruptions.

 

Can I access geo-restricted content like Netflix or Hulu?

Absolutely. With 80+ server locations worldwide, you can securely access global content wherever you are.
